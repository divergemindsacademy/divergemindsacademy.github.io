Meet the Team

Mentors

Ankan Kar

Co-Founder
ISI and CMI Alumni

Ankan is a passionate mathematician, computer scientist, AI developer, and quant researcher. He is well known for his brilliance as a mentor in the educational community. He is an alumnus of both ISI and CMI. He specializes in Olympiad problem solving, competitive exam strategy, and helping students build deep conceptual understanding from the ground up. He co-founded Diverge Minds Academy with a vision to make high-quality math education accessible to every student.

He has mentored students who have achieved success in multiple competitive exams, including INMO, RMO, IOQM/PRMO, ISI BMath, BStat and ISI PG courses, JEE Main, and JEE Advanced. Some of his students have also qualified for INOI. He himself secured AIR 3 in ISI MTech CS, AIR 56 in ISI BMath, Rank 1799 in JEE Advanced, and AIR 285 in WBJEE. Apart from that, he was a Yandex Cup finalist with an international rank of 144 in 2022 and 173 in 2021, and achieved an ACM ICPC regional rank of 292. He is now dedicated to helping young minds achieve their dreams.

Nirjhar Nath

Co-Founder
CMI Alumni

Nirjhar is a CMI alumnus and co-founder of Diverge Minds Academy with a passion for mathematics, computer science, and problem solving.

He has worked as a Teaching Assistant and Grader at AoPS and has mentored students in mathematical olympiads. A three-time INMO qualifier, he has also cleared the entrance examinations for ISI BMath, CMI UG, and the CMI MSc CS program. He believes that with the right guidance and a teaching approach tailored to each student's needs, meaningful progress is possible for everyone.

Soumabha Roy

Co-Founder
CMI Alumni

Soumabha has a strong background in mathematics and a passion for teaching. As a co-founder, he focuses on curriculum design and mentoring students preparing for ISI, CMI, and other competitive exams. His patient and methodical teaching style has helped many students achieve remarkable results.

He is an MSc DS student at CMI. He graduated from CMI with a scholarship. He secured AIR 68 in ISI BStat and AIR 48 in IIT JAM MS. He has mentored over 100 students for Olympiads, ISI, and CMI. He is now teaching students how to achieve their goals and overcome any obstacles in their way.
